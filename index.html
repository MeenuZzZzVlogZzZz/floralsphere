Floral craft is a creative art form that involves designing and arranging flowers, leaves, and other natural materials into beautiful patterns and decorations. It is often used for making bouquets, garlands, wreaths, and decorative pieces for homes, events, and celebrations. Floral craft not only enhances aesthetic beauty but also allows individuals to express their creativity and appreciation for nature. Different colors, shapes, and textures of flowers are carefully combined to create visually pleasing designs. This craft can be practiced as a hobby or a profession, and it also has cultural significance in many traditions around the world.
